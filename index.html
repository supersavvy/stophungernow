about US!



Created in 2016, MSJ Stop Hunger Now is a club dedicated towards helping students realize the life-changing impact they can have on people who have been negatively affected by hunger and inadequate food distribution. Our club also tries to help raise awareness about these issues through public presentations and fundraisers. MSJ SHN is honored to help high school students realize their passion for selfless service to both the local community and the world.
